  • EVERYTHING STARTS SMALL

    Sixty one years ago a small town in Southern Oregon didn't have a bank, so the community came together and built one. Today, Umpqua is the West Coast's largest community bank, with over 400 locations and experts in everything from personal and business banking to commercial and home lending.

    Our roots, our lives, and our success are completely intertwined, and we all grow to the extent that we believe in one another. Wherever you're trying to go, we're here to help you get there.
